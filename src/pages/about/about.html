About About the sunny 16 rule

On a bright sunny day, with distinct shadows, the sunny 16 rule applies.

Soft shadows, easily seen with soft edges is weak, hazy sunlight and needs 1 stop more light or f11 rather than f16 at 1/ISO.

Cloudy bright days refer to days where shadows are barely visible, and they require 2 stops more light or f8 and 1/ISO.

Heavy overcast means NO SHADOWS and requires 3 stops more light or f5.6 and 1/ISO, the same as in the shade on a sunny day.

Open shade / sunset, no shadows, requires 4 stops more light or f4 and 1/ISO.
